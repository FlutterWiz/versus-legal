Introduction

Versus ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Authentication: Email address and profile picture from Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple
  • Username: Your chosen username (displayed anonymously)
  • User ID: Unique identifier for your account

2. Usage Data

  • Questions you vote on and your vote choices
  • Questions you create
  • Points, level, and statistics
  • Daily activity (swipes, gems earned)

3. Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage patterns and crash reports
  • Advertising ID (for personalized ads)

4. What We DON'T Collect

  • We don't access your contacts, photos, or location
  • We don't collect sensitive personal information (race, religion, health data)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Create your account, authenticate, display your profile, track your progress
  • AI Content Moderation: We use OpenAI's API to review submitted questions for compliance with our guidelines
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Communication: Send important updates and respond to support requests
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads through Google AdMob
