Delete Your Versus Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can permanently delete your Versus account and all associated data directly from the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Versus app on your device
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  4. Scroll down to Account section
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm your decision

⚠️ What Gets Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted within 30 days:

  • Your email address and authentication data
  • Your username and profile information
  • All questions you created
  • Your voting history and statistics
  • Your points, level, and leaderboard position
  • Your gem balance and swipe history
  • Your subscription status (if applicable)

This action cannot be undone.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • You will be immediately signed out of the app
  • Your account will be marked for deletion
  • All personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days
  • Your questions will be removed from the platform
  • If you have an active subscription, it will be cancelled (no refund for partial periods)

Need Help?

If you're unable to delete your account through the app, or if you have questions, contact us:

Email: contact@alperefesahin.com

Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request - Versus"

Include: Your username and email address associated with your account

We will process your request within 5 business days.

Alternative: Just Take a Break

If you're not sure about permanent deletion, consider:

  • Sign out: Simply log out and return anytime
  • Unsubscribe: Cancel premium subscription but keep your account
  • Disable notifications: Stop notifications in app settings
